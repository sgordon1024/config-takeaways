# Tally — onboarding (worked sample)

**App:** Tally, split shared expenses with friends.
**Feature in scope:** first-run onboarding.
**Not in scope:** settle-up payments, group management, notifications.

## The bet
A first-time user logs one shared expense and sees the split within 2 minutes of opening the app.

## Definition of good
- **Primary:** Day-1 activation = new users who log a first expense and view a split. Target 50%+.
- **Guardrails:** per-step drop-off under 20%; no rise in "how do I add an expense" support tickets.

## Who signs off
Founder (sponsor), brand owner, you (design lead). No legal or compliance for this one.
