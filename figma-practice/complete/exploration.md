# 3. Divergent exploration in Figma Make

**Do in Figma:** in Figma Make, fire three prompts in parallel, each constrained to the Tally kit plus your skills. Keep them side by side.

## Three directions
1. **Checklist** — "3 things to try" with a progress bar. Safe, a bit boring.
2. **Wizard** — one question per screen up to the first expense. Guided, more taps.
3. **Progressive** — drop them straight into an add-expense screen, teach in context. Fewest steps, riskiest.

## Curate
Kill **checklist** (teaches, does not activate) and **wizard** (too many steps for a 2-minute bet). Survivor: **progressive**. Hand-tune the split-reveal motion on the survivor; do not accept Make's first easing.

## Kill list (keep it)
Checklist, wizard. Reason logged so they do not creep back in review.
