# Sprout — onboarding (working)

**App:** Sprout, houseplant care reminders.
**Feature in scope:** first-run onboarding.
**Not in scope:** photo plant identification, community, shop.

## The bet
A new user adds their first plant and sees its watering schedule within 2 minutes of opening the app.

## Definition of good
- **Primary:** Day-1 activation = added a plant and viewed a schedule they trust. Target 55%.
- **Trust signal:** of users who see a schedule, the share who confirm it looks right (one-tap "looks right?"). Target 70%+. _Added after pressure-test: the risk is schedule trust, not speed._
- **Guardrails:** per-step drop-off under 20%; no rise in "how do I add a plant" support tickets.

## Who signs off
Founder (sponsor), brand owner, you (design lead).
